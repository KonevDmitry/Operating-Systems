Prevention is set of rules to avoid the deadlock. We assure that Mutual-Exclusion or hold-and-wait or no-preeemption or cirular wait is never satisfied. However, it might not be always possible to follow those rules.
Recovery helps to get rid of deadlock when it is happened by backing up to the safe states. However, it might work for a long time going back and forward.
Avoidance requires all needed information about resources usage.
